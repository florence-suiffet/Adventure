​Our advice in choosing your materials

​​Over the course of our various adventures, we have drawn up a list of the equipment we feel is essential. Obviously, this is an exhaustive list, and its sole purpose is to advise you.

​A hiking bag Several points should be carefully observed. First of all, you should try to find a light bag that allows for good compartmentalization of your belongings. Obviously, it must be easy to handle and offer ventilation.

A first aid kit Versatile, with good compartmentalization.

​A sleeping bag I draw your attention to this point, it is necessary to pay attention to the temperature advised on the bags.

​Shoes You need shoes with good grip, shock resistance and cushioning. Weight is also a consideration here.

​​Compass Easy to use, easy to carry and of course accurate.

​​​Bottle Two points are important, the comfort of the bottle and the maintenance. It is also important to look at the capacity.
